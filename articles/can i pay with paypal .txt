"To pay with Paypal, follow these steps:On the order page, confirm your products, quantity, and ship-to information. Enter any promotional codes you want to use and click Go To Checkout. When prompted to enter payment information, click on the Paypal tab. If you already have a card on file, you can click Change under Payment Method. Click on Place Order and you'll be redirected to the Paypal site. Log in with your PayPal account information. Review your order and payment details and click Continue. Your order will be placed, and you will see your confirmation number on the next page."
