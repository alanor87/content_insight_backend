"To apply the same background to all pages in your book:Click on any page that has the background you want to apply to all pages in your book. Click the More Button in the menu at the top of your screen and then selectUse Page Background For All Pagesfrom the Layout menu.Note: This editing feature applies the background to pages you have already created. Any new pages you add will use backgrounds from your theme. You can use the same command at any time to apply to additional pages you have added.More information on Backgrounds"
