"To save a background to your Favorites section:Click on the background area of any page, then click on the Backgrounds tab in the sidebar on the right.There will be some suggested backgrounds, or you can pick the Find Backgrounds button at the left. Once you locate one that you would like to save as a favorite, hover your mouse over the background, and you will see two icons, a Magnifier, and a Heart icon. Click on the Heart icon to designate it as a Favorite.When it's red, it's a Favorite, when it's blank, or white, it's not.To see the backgrounds you previously marked as Favorites, click the My Favorites option in the top right area of the window.If you decide you no longer want a background in the Favorites area, just hover over it and uncheck the Heart icon.More information on Backgrounds"
