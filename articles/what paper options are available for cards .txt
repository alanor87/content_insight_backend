"Our Cards are are available in two paper styles - Signature Cardstock and Premium Cardstock. Our Signature card stock is a 120 lb paper featuring a silky smooth finish in an ultrawhite shade. 15 pt. thickness, acid free archival quality. Our premium card stock is a 120 lb paper featuring a lush tactile eggshell finish in an ultrawhite shade. This very rigid paper is perfect for housing your inspired designs. 18 pt. thickness, acid free archival quality."
