"Although Picaboo does not have a specific crop tool, you can use the zoom tool and to enlarge and focus on a specific area of your photo. Click on the photo and then under theEdit Photooption you can selectCrop/Position Photoand adjust the zoom in the window that pops up.Using the crossed arrows, you can can then drag the photo to the area you want as the focus. If you zoom in so far that the image won't print well, you'll see the low resolution warning appear over your photo.More information about Photo Adjustment Tools"
