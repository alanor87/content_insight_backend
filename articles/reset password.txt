"To reset your password:Go to the Picaboo.com home page In the upper right hand corner, pull down on the drop down menu “My Account” Click on “Account Settings” This will bring you to the “My Account” landing page, where you can click on “Password” You will be asked for your current password Then you will be asked to type in your new password twice: first, to enter your new password and second, to verify it Click “Save"
