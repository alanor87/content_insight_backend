"To add or remove borders around the photos in my project:Select a photo in your project by clicking on it. From the Edit Photo tab on the left, click on the Border or Shadow options. You can check or uncheck the boxes in the Border and Shadow section to add or remove those from the selected image.Once you make a change to one of the photos, you can also click to Apply to: All Photos on this Page to add or remove those options from all other images on the page, or Apply to: All Photos in this Book to add or remove those options from all of the images in the project. For borders, you can also select the color by using the color chooser box and adjust the thickness of the border by clicking on the thickness gauge.Note: If you don't see the Shadow or Border options, you may need to scroll down in the Edit Photo sidebar.View more Photo Adjustment Tools"
