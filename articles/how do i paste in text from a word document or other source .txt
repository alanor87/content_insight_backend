"You can paste text into a caption box as follows:Copy the text from the original Word document, web page, or other source. Ctrl+C (or Command+C on a Mac) will copy the text once it has been selected. Click anywhere in the caption box to select it. Press Ctrl+V on your keyboard (or Command+V on a Mac) to paste in your text.If you're pasting in text with foreign characters, it's best to use Arial font."
