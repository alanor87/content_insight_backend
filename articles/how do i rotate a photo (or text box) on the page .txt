"To rotate the entire frame:Select the photo by clicking on it. Hover your mouse over the small box at the end of the blue line coming from the center top of the photo. Holding down the left mouse button on that box, drag your mouse to rotate the entire image frame. You can then resize the frame to fit your photo (or widen a text box) by selecting any of the available handles and dragging to change the dimensions.Note: Text boxes will only be able to be narrowed or widened.The height will automatically depend on the size and amount of text in the box.To rotate a photo within the frame:Select the photo by clicking on it. In the Edit sidebar, underneath the image, click one of the circular arrows to turn your photo 90 degrees in either direction."
