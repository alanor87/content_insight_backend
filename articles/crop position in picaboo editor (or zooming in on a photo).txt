"Sometimes you may want to crop an image, or move it's position within the frame, to better highlight the most important part of the image. To begin, click on your image, then the Crop/Position button on the left. From there, you can use the slider to zoom in on the photo and the crossed arrows to move the photo's position in the frame.Once you've completed all changes you'd like to make, you can click to Cancel the change or Apply the changes you have made to the image."
